Aqua Farm Tourism

Resorts to escape for busy life, experience farm life, learn some basics of aqua farming, relax, breathe abundant oxygen, refresh yourself and enjoy natural and hut life at a rural pristine & beautiful location. Hugua aqua farm is located at Mirjan, Kumta in Karwar district. It is near to Gokarna and Mirjan fort, on the banks of the Aghanashini River, a branch of the Sharavati.
