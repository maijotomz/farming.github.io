Mud crab and Soft shell crab

Hugua Marine has been harvesting and processing top quality mud crabs, softshell crabs, vannamei and tiger prawns. Hugua Marine is located at one of the best natural habitat for these species, where the Aghanashini river meets the Arabian sea. We are the first and largest producers of mud crabs in Karnataka India. We buy best quality mud crab seeds from Rajiv Gandhi Centre for Aquaculture which is the Research & Development arm of the Marine Products Export Development Authority (MPEDA). Healthy Mud Crab Seed free from diseases produced at bio-secure hatchery facility using healthy, screened parent stocks. After very stringent health and disease related testing, seed is then transported from hatcheries to our farm in Kumta to grow as fleshy mud crabs and softshell crabs.

Mud Crab

Delicious large mud crabs or Green mangrove crabs (Scylla Serrata) popular all over the work for its taste are scientifically grown.

Soft Shell Crab

Mud crabs from growout farms are handpicked, separately cultured in boxes and taken out during molting process, cleaned and packed for marketing as delicious soft shell.

Vannamei and Tiger prawn

We buy best quality Vannamei and tiger prawn seeds from the most renowned Hatcheries After very stringent health and disease related testing, seed is then transported from hatcheries to our farm in Kumta. Hugua marine farms are equipped with facilities for Bio-Security, and Aeration Systems. Absolutely no steroids or antibiotics are used in our farm ensuring that all our produce is totally hygienic, safe and secure, thereby producing the best farm fresh quality delicious crabs & shrimps.
